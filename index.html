Alpinism

Mountain climbing(also can be called Alpinism) is a thrilling and challenging activity that combines physical endurance, mental fortitude, and a deep appreciation for nature. Scaling towering peaks, climbers often face unpredictable weather, rugged terrain, and thin air, which demand both preparation and resilience. The sense of accomplishment upon reaching a summit is unparalleled, offering breathtaking views and a profound connection to the natural world.

Four main sites for mountain climbing are

Mount Everest, Nepal/Tibet

K2, Pakistan/China

Mount Kilimanjaro, Tanzania

Denali, USA
